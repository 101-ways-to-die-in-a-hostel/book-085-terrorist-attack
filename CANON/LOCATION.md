# Location Profile

## The Hostel

**Name:** Old Town Budget Hostel
**Type:** No-frills budget hostel
**Star Rating:** 2.5
**Price Range:** $14-28 per night

### History
Opened in 2010 in a renovated apartment building. Family-run, practical, no pretensions. Popular with budget travelers for its central location and honest value.

### Architecture
- **Style:** Renovated 19th-century apartment building
- **Floors:** 4
- **Total beds:** 45
- **Notable features:** Central location, basic but clean, working kitchen

### Layout
```
Ground Floor:
- Reception
- Small common room with TV
- Kitchen

Floors 1-3:
- 6-bed mixed dorms
- 4-bed dorms
- Private double rooms
- Shared bathrooms per floor

Floor 4:
- Staff apartment
```

### The Death Location (Specific)
**Not at the hostel.** Stefan dies at a restaurant 1.2 km from the hostel, in a local neighborhood outside the main tourist zone. The hostel becomes a gathering place for mourners and media after his identity emerges.
